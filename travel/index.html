Travel

The closest airport to the Cape is Boston Logan. Flights leave regularly from all London airports, with more affordable options going via Ireland and Iceland.

You can also fly into New York and get the train from Penn Station. It is a very pretty train journey and takes about 4 and a half hours. Train tickets can be booked at amtrack.com

Flight prices are fluctuating so good to set up alerts for the best deals on sites such as kayak.com, skyscanner.com and expedia.com.

Once you have your travel plans confirmed do let us know and we will coordinate carpooling etc from Boston to the Cape.
