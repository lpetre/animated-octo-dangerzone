Travel

The closest airport to the Cape is Boston Logan. Flights leave regularly from all London airports, with more affordable options going via Ireland and Iceland.

You can also fly into New York and get the train from Penn Station to Boston South Station. It is a very pretty train journey and takes about 4 and a half hours. Train tickets can be booked at amtrak.com.

Flight prices are fluctuating so good to set up alerts for the best deals on sites such as kayak.co.uk, skyscanner.co.uk and expedia.co.uk.

Once you have your travel plans confirmed do let us know and we will coordinate carpooling etc from Boston to the Cape.

Things to do

Cape Cod is a lovely holiday destination. The town of Chatham itself is a small beach resort with restaurants, bars, gift shops, boating, fishing, beaches, biking, golf, wildlife and more!

Plus there are tons of places on the Cape to visit, including Martha’s Vineyard, Nantucket, and Provincetown.

There is even whale watching and seal tours!
