Travel

The closest airport to the Cape is Boston Logan. Flights leave regularly from all London airports, with more affordable options going via Ireland and Iceland. You can also fly into New York and get the train from Penn Station. It is a very pretty train journey and takes about 4 and a half hours. Train tickets can be booked here: www.amtrak.com Flight prices are fluctuating so good to set up alerts for the best deals on sites such as kayak, skyskanner and Expedia.
